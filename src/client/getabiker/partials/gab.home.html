Welcome

Our objective is to connect individuals through our web platform so they can provide themselves a delivery service based on trust and reputation.

How it works

Every member of GAC can create delivery tickets and / or accomplish exiting ones.

Being a GAC member means that you have reputation and reviews from the people you interact with.

Pricing is defined by the registered coursiers and his skills. Our price model is a flat 0.05% over ticket or the minimum to cover the payment proccessing fee set by stripe. We are 100% sure that this platform will provide the cheapest service in Paris.

Who are we

We are Manitas, a bunch of people of different nationalities that create and expand projects related to social and solidarity economy. GAC will try to cover several aspects in these subjects.
