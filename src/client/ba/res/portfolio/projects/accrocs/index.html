Description

Acrocs is a gastronomic blog where you can find excellents reviews about lots of 'must taste' restaurants around differents corners of Paris.

Why

There are several blogs and web site specialized in gastronomy and even focused in Paris. When you are looking for reviews written by passionated people you can only list a few. The author of this blog hundred of restaurants over the last years and she is doing it for pleasure.

How

A gastronomic blog where you can examine specialized reviews. You are able to like or dislike a review as well as commenting. You have a way to contact the author at any time and you can even send personal reviews to be published on the blog.

Essence

Its time to discover new options to eat in Paris, not just the turistic side of paris but to inmerse in the culture an examine small restaurants dispersed in hidden corners of the city. You will find not only the best local dishes but really accesible prices.
