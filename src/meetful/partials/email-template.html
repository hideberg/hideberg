Meetful allows you, via its platform, to organize and participate to many events in the city where you live or where you are traveling. You will be able to meet new friends by cooking together, sharing a meal and having a good time !

Meetful events are free. The idea is that everyone contributes to the event by bringing something or helping with the preparation...we let you figure with the other participants how you want to organize your event !
