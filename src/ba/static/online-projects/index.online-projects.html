Diagnostical.fr

Diagnostical is a reservation platform for real estate diagnostics already implemented in many departments (50 independent diagnosticians in metropolitan France). Founded by a diagnostician and a web entrepreneur, this startup tends to literally revolutionize the profession of real estate diagnoser by a very simple process, that of digitization and uberization.
If you are a Diagnoser: Offer your expertise on Diagnostic, and you will gain new customers quickly, regularly and free of charge.
If you are the owner: You can access the certified diagnostic schedules available in your department and at the right price
